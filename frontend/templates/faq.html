About TablesAlive.com

What is TablesAlive.com?

TablesAlive.com is a powerful tool designed to bring your static Google Sheets data to life. It transforms raw data into interactive dashboards, visualizations, and analytical tools without writing a single line of code.

Is it free to use?

Yes, the core features of TablesAlive.com are currently free to use. We aim to provide accessible data visualization for everyone.

Features

Can I visualize my data?

Absolutely! You can create various charts including Bar, Line, Scatter, Pie, Area, and Histograms using the 'Visualization' section.

Can I filter and group my data?

Yes. Use the 'Data Tools' section to rename columns, apply complex filters (Numeric, Date, Categorical), and group data by specific fields with aggregation functions.

Does it support real-time updates?

The dashboard fetches the latest data from your Google Sheet whenever you click 'Refresh Now' in the sidebar.

Support
